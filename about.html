Grace Chen

who's that?

Grace just graduated high school in San Diego and it starting college at Brown University in Providence, RI in the fall. She is passionate about illustration and design and hopes to pursue a career in UI/UX or software development and eventually wants to settle down in a small nation state of her own. Apart from her plans for world domination, she enjoys scribbling on a screen, shopping for snacks, and people-watching.

Career so far

After building a foundation in sketching and painting, Grace ventured into digital art, eventually finding a home in design and illustration.
